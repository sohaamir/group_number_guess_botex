You will be playing a number guessing game for {{ NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds in a group of {{ PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }}.

How to Play:
  • In each round, you will guess a number between 0 and 100.
  • You will have 10 seconds to make your guess.
  • A random target number will be generated.
  • After you submit your guess, you will need to wait for the 10 second limit to expire before starting the next round and making your next guess.
  • Your score for each round is the absolute difference between your guess and the target number.
  • If you don't answer within the time limit, a value of 100 will be assigned as your guess.
  • The player with the lowest total score (closest guesses) across all rounds will win.
When you are ready, press the next button. You will be placed in a waiting room for a group to be formed.
